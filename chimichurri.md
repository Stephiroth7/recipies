## JUDY'S MEATLOAF

**INGREDIENTS**

* 1 cup cilantro
* 1 flat leaf or Italian Parsely
* 1/2 cup olive oil
* 4 tbsp lemon juice
* 5 cloves garlic
* 1/4 small onion or one shallot
* 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
* 2 tsp oregano
* 2 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp black pepper
* 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes


**PREPARATION**

1. use a food processor to finely chop all the ingredients.
