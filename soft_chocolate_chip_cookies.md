# Soft Chocolate Chip Cookies
from Sally's Cookie Addiction

* Makes app'x. 36 Cookies
* Requires chilling dough min 2 hours

**INGREDIENTS**
* 2 ¼ (270 g) cups all purpose flour
* 1 ½ teaspoon cornstarch
* ½ - 1 teaspoon salt (personal preference)
* 1 teaspoon baking soda

* ¾ cup (1 ½ sticks or 180 g) unsalted butter, melted and slightly cooled
* ¾ cup (170 g) packed light brown sugar
* ½ cup (100 g) granulated white sugar
* 2 large eggs: 1 whole egg plus 1 yolk, room temperature
* 1 tablespoon (20 g) dark or unsulphured molasses
* 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
* 1 ⅓ cups (240 g) semi-sweet chocolate chips



**PREPARATION**

Preheat oven to 325F
Cover half sheet baking pan in parchment paper 

* Whisk flour, baking soda, corn starch and salt together and set aside.
* In a large bowl, use a hand mixer to mix together the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until no sugar lumps remain.
* Add the eggs and vanilla and beat until combined. 
* Add the previously mixed dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, and combine on low speed. Scrape the sides of the bowl and continue beating to make sure it's all combined.
  * the dought will be soft and very wet, it might be easier to mix by hand with a spatula.
* Add the chocolate chips while mixing.
* Cover, and chill the dough for at least 2 hours and up to 4 days. If chilling longer than 3 hours, let the dough come to room temp for about 30 minutes before using.
* Scoop approximately 2 tablespoons of dough per cookie and place 2 inches apart on a lined baking sheet
  *  **or slightly smaller 1.5 tbsp cookies, a purple #40 scoop**
* Bake 12 - 13 minutes until lightly browned on the sides - the centers will look soft.
* Cool on a wire rack for 5 minutes after removing from oven

NOTES:
* Freeze the individual cookie balls and bake directly from frozen at 300 super convection for 13 - 15 minutes.


STORAGE:
* Unbaked, unassembled cookies can be stored in the freezer in an airtight container.
* Baked & cooled cookies can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 1 week.
