BUTTERMILK SORBET

INGREDIENTS: 
2 cups low-fat buttermilk
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup light corn syrup
 
PREPARATION:

To prepare sorbet, combine buttermilk, sugar, and corn syrup in a medium bowl, stirring until the sugar dissolves. Pour mixture into the freezer can of an ice-cream freezer; freeze according to manufacturer's instructions. Spoon sorbet into a freezer-safe container; cover and freeze 1 hour or until firm.
