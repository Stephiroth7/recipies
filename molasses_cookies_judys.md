# JUDY'S MOLASSES COOKIES
Makes A LOT of cookies

TO DO: scale these measurements for weight and/or halve them

## INGREDIENTS

* 1 ½ cups (or 1 ½ sticks) Crisco shortening
* 2 Cups white granulated sugar
* ½ Cup dark unsulphured Molasses
* 2 Eggs
* 4 Cups all-purpose flour
* 4 tsp baking soda
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp ground ginger
* 1 tsp ground cinnamon
* 1 tsp ground cloves

## PREPARATION

* Mix first four ingredients. Sift dry ingredients together and add to creamed ingredients.
* Scoop into balls the size of a small walnut, or use the orange or pink scoop. 
* Roll balls in sugar.
* Place about 1 inch apart on a parchment lined baking sheet. Cookies will flatten and crack on top as they bake.
* Bake for 8-10 minutes at 350º in the middle rack of the oven.
* Take them out when the first crack is visible and let cool on a wire rack
